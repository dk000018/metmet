How It's Done

Formula of Weight = Density x Volume

W = ρ x V kg

Density ( ρ ) : The density of the metals are already preloaded in kg/m3

Volume of a cuboid (which is rectangle here) = Length x Width x Height

V = L x w x h mm3

Volume of a cylinder = Diameter x Height

V = π r2 h mm3

The mm3 is then converted into m3

∴ W = ρ x V kg
